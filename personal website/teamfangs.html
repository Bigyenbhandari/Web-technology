TEAM FANGS

FC Fangs Logo

Branching from another team called Axis, Fangs started out with just 4 founding members- Bigyen Bhandari, Pranav Hada, Aaranya Lal Maskey and Alish Malla. It all started in the Covid-19 lockdown, as a small Esports team. Since then, Fangs has expanded to not just in Esports, but it has its own Football team, basketball team, and is involved in many more fields.

As of right now, Fangs has over 20 members, with 9 of them being the Core members.
